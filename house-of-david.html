Project Details

House of David

Overview

House of David was a fashion campaign created for WearJireh, a Christian fashion brand. The collection drew inspiration from the biblical story of King David, using visual direction, styling, and campaign assets to convey faith, leadership, and worship.

As creative lead, I managed visual direction, styled the collection, produced imagery, and oversaw the social rollout to bring the campaign to market.
