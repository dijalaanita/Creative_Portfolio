Project Details

Rendezvous

Overview

Rendezvous was an editorial fashion project created in collaboration with photographer and creative director Naren. It explored visual storytelling through carefully curated styling, composition, and character-driven fashion narratives.

As the stylist, I developed and executed the wardrobe direction for the shoot, styling six complete looks that aligned with the creative vision and strengthened the project's narrative.

Responsibilities

  • Outfit Planning
  • Wardrobe Coordination
  • Fittings
  • Look Development
  • Accessory Styling
  • On-Set Styling Support

Deliverables

  • Styling Direction
  • Fitting Documentation
